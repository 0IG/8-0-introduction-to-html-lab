The Perfect PB&J

Description

This peanut butter and jelly sandwich is my favorite sandwhich. It has the perfect balance of ingredients and looks great when made right

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of white bread
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1 jar of creamy peanut butter
  • 1 butter knife
  • A sharp knife
  • A cutting board

Directions

  1. Lay both slices of bread next to each othr on a cutting board
  2. With the butter knife, spread a 1/8 inch layer of peanut butter on the left side
  3. On the right side, spread a 1/8 inch layer of jelly.
  4. Carefully place the two halves together so that the jelly is on top.
  5. With a sharp knife, carefully cut the sandwich in half.
